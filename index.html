Welcome to Al Zaam

We are focuses on creating the highest standards in producing breads and pastries and as a result, won the hearts of faithful customers in Jeddah. Al Zaam Bakery is a leader in the baking industry, known for its leading brand, innovative products, freshness and quality. Due to the tremendous success in Jeddah, we are planning a vast expansion across the Kingdom. Al Zaam Bakery has constructive ideas and ambitious plans.
